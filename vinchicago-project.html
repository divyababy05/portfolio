Vinchicago

Redesign E-commerce website for wine products

THE CHALLENGE

Vin-Chicago is a platform e-commerce website for wine products. As UI designer on this project, I chose to help Vin-Chicago redesign their website. I just want to work on a responsive site and redesign its look and feel. To begin with, I created a mood board from which I took inspiration to choose the colors and typography while trying to keep part of the previous website graphic chart. Then I developed all the screens on the Adobe XD software following the atomic design method. About the colors choice, like colors on the website, there are colors of Green as per existence. So with a shade of monochromatic color scheme of green choose them randomly. I kept the same font which is readable and easy to use. I have of course to do the desirability and usability test with the users to adapt.
