Project Summary

The virtual personal trainer app is a fitness application that features a virtual personal trainer who guides users through their workout using a mirror and a wrist band. The app provides users with personalised workout plans based on their fitness goals and helps them track their progress.

Diagrams - User Journey Map

Additionally, the app could use data from the wrist band to track the user's heart rate, calories burned, and other metrics. This information could be used to adjust the user's workout routine and provide personalized recommendations for achieving their fitness goals. Overall, a virtual personal trainer app could be a useful tool for individuals who prefer to workout at home or don't have access to a physical personal trainer. However, it's important to keep in mind that the effectiveness of the app will depend on the quality of the virtual personal trainer's guidance and the accuracy of the wrist band's data.

Design Systems - Color , Typography

Overall, incorporating these design elements can make the virtual personal trainer app more visually appealing and engaging for users.

Wireframe - Design Iterations

The application will have a home screen that displays the current exercise session as well as a list of connected trainers. There will be a tab for the trainer to specify about there connection, as well as a button to view the trainer's schedule, which will contain a time slot and date to confirm a chat or video call for nutritional advice. The details screen for analytics will show your body output such as target goal, water to drink, and sleep analyst. There will also be a button that allows you to join a live exercise session with Accessible Exercise right away.

Prototypes - Interaction Models

Overall, the user journey with the virtual personal trainer app involves setting up a personalized workout plan, completing workout sessions, tracking progress, and receiving support and motivation from other users. The app aims to provide users with a comprehensive and interactive fitness experience to help them achieve their fitness goals.
