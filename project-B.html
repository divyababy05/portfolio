Project Summary

A digital platform aimed at reducing plastic dependency by suggesting alternative options and lifestyle choices, with potential for additional features tailored to user preferences.

Diagrams - Crazy 8

A digital platform aimed at reducing plastic-dependent lifestyles and promoting sustainable alternatives. The platform provides various features and functionalities to support users in adopting eco-friendly practices. The design focuses on offering alternative solutions to common plastic-dependent activities, such as shopping, food storage, and personal care. It provides a user-friendly interface that educates and guides users towards sustainable choices.

Design Systems - Color , Typography

A design system for the digital platform would provide a set of consistent design elements, patterns, and guidelines that ensure a cohesive and intuitive user experience.

Mockup - Mobile Application

The mobile app provides a digital platform to promote a lifestyle that avoids dependency on plastic. It includes features such as a Barcode Scanner to identify plastic products, a Community Forum for users to share alternatives, and Virtual Challenges to encourage sustainable habits.

Mockup - Website

A digital platform aiming to promote a lifestyle free from plastic dependency can offer various features and functionalities based on user needs. These may include a website explaining the concept and purpose, product recommendations for plastic-free alternatives, a community forum for discussions and sharing ideas, and educational resources to raise awareness about sustainable living choices.
